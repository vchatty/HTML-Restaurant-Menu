About Us

Origin Story

Started in Miami, Florida, we are a small team of passionate and classically trained chefs who got together and created a concept where food and art come together. Founded in 1986, H Thai ML has served thousands of patrons and made memories to last a lifetime.

We are dedicated to creating both traditional and fusion Thai dishes that tantalize your tastebuds and leave a lasting impression.
